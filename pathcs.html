Roadmap to Cyber Security

  • Step 1
    Foundations and Basics
    Establish a strong understanding of networking fundamentals, protocols, and security principles in the first month. Dive into basic programming and scripting languages, such as Python, in the second month.
  • Step 2
    Introduction to Cybersecurity
    Explore basic cybersecurity concepts, including cryptography and secure communications, in the first two weeks. Focus on understanding common cyber threats and vulnerabilities, as well as risk management.
  • Step 3
    Intermediate Skills
    Learn about penetration testing and ethical hacking techniques in the first two weeks. Dive into security operations, incident response, and monitoring for the next two months.
  • Step 4
    Specialization and Advanced Topics
    Choose a specialization such as network security, application security, or cloud security in the first two weeks. Spend the next months on advanced topics like threat intelligence, advanced persistent threats (APTs), and security assessments.
  • Step 5
    Advanced Projects and Networking
    Develop and execute advanced cybersecurity projects, simulating real-world scenarios in the first two weeks. Engage with the cybersecurity community, participate in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, and expand your professional network in the remaining weeks.
